about us

TCG Industries is both a family owned and operated, Los Angeles based Umbrella Company housing some of the most reputable cannabis brands in the country. Facilitating industry specific needs with well over 55 years of experience in cultivating high grade cannabis. TCG was founded to meet the needs of the rapidly developing regulated cannabis industry. Services include but are not limited to designing and operating cultivation facilities with up to 200 lights in addition to developing cannabis strains in the greater Los Angeles area.

The Cannabis Gardner and Payasogrow are the pioneer brands that are named after the master growers of the facilities that produce the cannabis flower, in which all of the following brands are sourced from. Black Gold RSO, as the brand name suggests, offers CBD and RSO (Rick Simpson Oil, a concentrated form of cannabis oil) to clientele for medical benefits. Cannaessentials is the topical line with products varying from a healing salve to facial serums, giving TCG industries its serene touch.

Collectively the brands have developed over ten top winning strains, produced some of the most consistent and high-quality cannabis genetics, with several sought after cross breeds and many more to come. Considering the wealth of experience that built TCG industries and the rapid growth within the cannabis industry, business partners and master growers, Philip and Terence Cabral brought in sisters, Maria and Paulette Cabral as a beneficial addition to the team. Together they are primarily focused on legality, infrastructure, expansion and contracting, rounding out the executive team in totality. With the expertise in toe, TCG Industries also offer end-to-end consulting services exclusively for large scale operations.

To date the highest THC value grown has been up to 34% and the team has collected two High Times Cannabis Cup Awards. Specializing in indoor and outdoor pesticide free, and all organic practices. With decades of experience in several mediums and growing environments from soilless, soil, coco, etc. TCG Industries passion for helping people combined with their industry specific talent and experience are what truly differentiates them from many other cannabis producers. They are known for developing award-winning product that remains in the highest of demand throughout a distribution network that encompasses the world.
